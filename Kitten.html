Kitten Care

Learn about how to care for your new Kitty

Feeding

  • Buy a good brand of kitten food, Friskies is our reccomended brand, but there are many good ones out there
  • Feed your new Kitty a mix of wet and dry food, and while treats are not necessary they are certainly appreciated!
  • We reccommend only feeding your cat twice a day; morning and night

Clean Up

  • Buy good sized litter boxes with any litter of your choice, fill the box about one third of the way
  • Clean the litter box out once a day, every other week empty out all the litter and give it a good scrub
  • We reccomend keeping one litter box on every floor of the house

Other

  • Play with your kitten regularly, go to your local pet store and pick out some toys that you and your kitten will enjoy playing with!
  • Regularly pet and spend time with your kitten, they need a little TLC just like you and me
  • Want to do even more for your cat? Try finding them a companion!
  • Note: remember that you are required to spay or neuter your new fuzzy companion
